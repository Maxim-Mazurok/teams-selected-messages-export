Teams Selected Messages Export

This extension works inside the Microsoft Teams web page. All export buttons appear on the Teams page itself, not in this popup.

How to use:

  1. Open teams.microsoft.com or teams.cloud.microsoft and sign in
  2. Navigate to any chat or channel
  3. Click the Export button in the Teams title bar (top-right corner)
  4. The export panel with all buttons (Copy MD, Clear Selection, Download MD, Download HTML, Full chat MD, Full chat HTML) will appear inside the Teams page

If you don't see the Export button in the Teams title bar, try reloading the Teams page.
